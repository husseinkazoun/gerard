About

Award-winning filmmaker and musician based in Beirut, Lebanon. My work explores the intersection of storytelling, sound, and social expression. As director of "The Stranger Within Me" and an active figure in Beirut's electronic music scene, I craft visual and sonic experiences that reflect real-life complexities and aim to connect with audiences on a personal level.
